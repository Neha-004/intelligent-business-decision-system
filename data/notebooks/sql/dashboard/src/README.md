## Project Overview
This project builds an end-to-end **data-driven decision support system** using real-world retail data.  
The goal is to transform raw business data into **actionable insights** that help stakeholders make
better decisions around sales, profit, customers, and operations.

The project follows an **industry-style analytics workflow**:
- Data understanding & cleaning
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- SQL-based business queries
- Dashboard-ready insights
- Final recommendations for decision-makers

---

## Dataset
**Global Superstore Dataset**  
A widely used retail dataset containing:
- Orders
- Customers
- Products
- Sales
- Profit
- Regions & segments

---

## Business Questions Answered
- Which product categories and sub-categories drive the most profit?
- Which regions and states are underperforming?
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- How does discounting impact profitability?
- What actions can improve revenue and reduce losses?
